** +
** =aiSuspend= removes symlinks and =.claude/= entries and renames =AI-DevStatus.org= / =AI-WorkPlan.org= to a =.dormant= suffix so the collaboration state survives.
** =aiResume= restores the =.dormant= files, re-installs the symlinks and =.claude/=, and infers the activity from =AI-Activity.org= or the =Activity:= header of =AI-WorkPlan.org=.
** Both commands operate on the current directory --- no =--root= parameter needed.
** -B|
